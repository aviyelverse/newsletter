Aviyel is a community platform for open source projects to monetise and be sustainable. It helps to integrate and bring together multiple platforms in one location. Aviyel gives you the tools you need to talk about, define, track, and evolve your product idea into an Open Source project that developers love. Contributors and specialists can also join the community, participate, and be adequately rewarded. It also provides a direct line of connection with the community, ensuring that you receive guaranteed assistance from discovery to implementation and beyond.
